Dangers & Safety Tips

Improper handling of acids and bases can lead to dangerous accidents. Follow these tips:

  • Always wear protective gloves and eyewear when handling chemicals.
  • Store acids and bases in separate, labeled containers.
  • Neutralize acids and bases before disposal.
